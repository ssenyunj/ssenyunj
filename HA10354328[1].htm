View the Privacy Statement

The Microsoft Office 2010 Privacy Statement is available only online at Privacy Statement Highlights for Microsoft Office 2010.

View the privacy statement from within an Office program

  1. Click the File tab.
  2. Under Help, click Options.
  3. Click Trust Center.
  4. Under Protecting your privacy, click the link for the Privacy Statement.

Search Office.com

  1. Make sure your computer is connected to the Internet.
  2. In the main window of your Microsoft Office 2010 program, press F1 to open Help.
  3. On the Connection Status menu, which is located at the bottom of the Help window, click Show content from Office.com.
  4. In the Type words to search for box, type privacy statement.
